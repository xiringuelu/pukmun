Moderation Rules

In Pukmun we trust you!

You are the one deciding which recipes are published and which are going to the recipe hell.

Please be gentle and don't discard any recipes just because it's not your cup of tea (there is always a recipe for everyone).

Reasons to reject a recipe:

  • There is no photo, it is too small, has strange proportions or its quality is too low.
  • The name of the recipe is wrong or has a typo.
  • The category is wrong.
  • The tags are not descriptive. They are not propperly formated (all words together, etc.)
  • Any typos or strange symbols in the ingredients or steps.
  • Any other deliverated error.

Thanks for your help.
