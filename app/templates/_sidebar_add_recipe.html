Important Info

All recipes are moderated by the Pukmun community, that means that nothing goes online unless a certain number of users accept it. That's why it's very important the quality of the content submitted.

Users expect a recipe with an original photo or the final result of your recipe with a good size and decent quality (large images are reduced automatically, so do not worry about that).

Tell us about this recipe, how it's related with you and your experiences and memories with cooking and eating. A good description will catch the eye of the pukmuns (and they are the one deciding if it's approved).

Please categorise the recipe, and add a few tags in order to be able to find it effortlessly. Remember to user the right format (separated by a comma).

Include all the ingredients needed for the amount of portions indicated and don't be afraid of entering into much detail in the preparation steps, all information is good for us!

You can add links in your recipe, but it's not very recommended to add too many.

Last but not least, pay attention to the redaction and try to avoid typos.

Thank you and good luck!
