Register Information

Please register to Pukmun through this form completing all the fields.

Your username will be used to log into the web and it will be the name displayed to other users. You can change your username at a later time. You will also be able to log in with your e-mail address.

Your email won't be visible to the community and we promise that we won't send you any spam emails. It will be only used for safety purposes (to reset your password if necessary). Once registered you will receive a confirmation email in order to activate your account.

If the username or email are already in use you will receive an alert and the registration process will be cancelled. If you have lost access to your account and cannot recover it via resetting your password, please contact us and we will do it for you.
